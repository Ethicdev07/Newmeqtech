About Us


Newmeq Technologies Ltd. is an IT company that is incorporated with Corporate
Affairs Commission (CAC). We develop highly intuitive and enterprise software
products and services for private and public sectors.
Our core expertise has been in development of web and mobile solutions for
companies,schools, colleges and other establishments so as to make life easier
for people. Our products and services are designed and developed to meet up
with the international standards while taking strict cognisance into our
indigenous operations. They are developed as a result of extensive research on
Nigerian systems.
